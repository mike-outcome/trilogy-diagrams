Your Process Today

How the monthly budget-variance review runs now, through Yardi and Canopy.

The Data

Monthly Financials

Each property's actuals live in Yardi; budgets are set annually. 16 properties.

Who's Involved

PMs & Asset Team

On-site property managers review flagged lines; your asset-management team oversees.

How It Arrives

Yardi → Canopy

Financials flow automatically into Canopy each month, around the 18th–20th.

What Happens

Flag & Explain

Canopy highlights accounts more than 10% and $250 off budget. PMs type an explanation for each by hand.

The Result

QBR Inputs

Explanations feed quarterly reviews. Multi-month trends and reminders are handled manually.
