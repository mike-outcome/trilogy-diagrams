02

Understanding This POC

So expectations are clear — here is exactly what the proof of concept is, what it is not yet, and how to use it.

What it is

A working proof — real product, real data.
  • A live, working application — not slides or a mockup.
  • Built on your real data — actual Velo Village financials across several months.
  • The complete workflow — from raw monthly file to flagged variances, comments, trends, and AI Q&A.
  • Yours to explore — log in and click through it yourself.

What it isn't yet

Deliberately out of scope for a proof — added at rollout.
  • Not connected to live Yardi — the monthly file was loaded manually for the POC.
  • One property, not all 16 — proof on Velo Village only.
  • No property-manager logins yet — on-site teams are onboarded at rollout.
  • No automated reminders yet — the emails that nudge PMs come with the full build.

How to use it

Open the POC Workspace
Your Outcome contact will share your secure login link.
  • Open your workspace using the link above.
  • Pick a property and month from the filters.
  • Review the flagged variances that need an explanation.
  • Add or refine a comment and watch the completion tracker update.
  • Open a trend to see a line item move across months.
  • Ask Octo a question — e.g., “top 5 expense variances this month.”
03

The Path to a Full Rollout

A clear, staged path from this single-property proof to a fully automated system across your portfolio. Each phase removes a manual step and adds coverage.

1

Confirm scope & thresholds

Lock the properties in scope and the variance thresholds that flag a line (percentage and dollar), and align on the approach so we build to your standard.

2

Connect your data Automation

Automate delivery of the monthly files straight from Yardi (e.g., a secure SFTP feed) so there's no manual upload — for every property, every month.

3

Bring your team in Onboarding

Onboard property managers with their own secure logins to review and enter variance comments directly in Outcome — replacing the manual entry step in Canopy.

4

Turn on reminders Workflow

Automated emails nudge property managers about any outstanding variance explanations, so every flagged line is addressed before the month closes.

5

Go portfolio-wide Scale

Roll out to all 16 properties with portfolio roll-up views built for your quarterly business reviews.

Sequencing and timing are confirmed together during scoping — this is the shape of the build, not a fixed schedule.
